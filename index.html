Features

1. Find the best Moove
See a list of all your city's bars and clubs in order of most Mooves made. Get the scoop on the place by reading the anonymous comments left by other Moovers to help decide if its going to be your next moove.

2. Leave reviews
Is the band killing it where you are at? Let other Moovers know!

3. Uber Estimates
Cool, so you've found your Moove - now how are you going to get there? Moove will show you the price estimate of an Uber from your current location, as well how soon you can get picked up.
