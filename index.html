How does Moove work?

To start, just select an area in your city you are looking to go to and click ’Find Mooves’. From there you will be shown a list of all the bars in the area in the order of highest number of positive reviews.

How is this different than Yelp or FourSquare?

Apps like Yelp and FourSquare are great if you want to look up an establishment to find out whats the best menu item they offer , or to write a review about how awesome ( or terrible :/ ) your waitress was.

The purpose behind Moove however, is to give you an as live as it gets feed on what’s happening NOW at each location; helping you decide whether or not you should make that moove. Maybe the guy/girl ratio is or isn’t on point, or the bartender with the black hat is being quite generous with his pours - whatever the case may be, we want to keep you informed to help you make the best bar-hopping decisions.

Oh, and we also don’t require you to “socially connect” to use Moove. In fact, we don’t even ask for your name or email.

Okay, so what exactly are Moove Reviews?

Reviews are made solely by users of the Moove app, and show the total count of up and down votes an establishment has for the night, as well as a live feed of the latest three comments from other Moovers.

How do I leave a review?

Leaving a review is easy. Users can rate an establishment they are currently at or just visited, by leaving an optional anonymous blurb about the place, and simply suggesting other Moovers to ‘Moove On’ or ‘Make Mooves’. All reviews are published immediately and added to the live stream, as well as tallied and shown as either an up/down vote in the establishments popularity counter for the night.

Cool, anything else?

In addition to showing users a live feed of reviews, the Moove app also displays a cost and time estimate of an Uber ride from their current location to each destination.
